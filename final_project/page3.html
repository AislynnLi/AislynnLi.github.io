Third Goal

What? Designing radicals doesn't satisfy you anymore? Fine...I guess it's time for you to design your own Chinese characters!

The third goal is to build your own Chinese characters based on the meanings of radicals. If it happens to exist, congratulations! If it doesn't, write a letter to the Chinese committee. It might come into being next year...

Instructions

  • Hover on radical buttons to get the meanings of radicals
  • Press radical buttons to get the radicals
  • Drag radicals to "spell" a character
  • Drag the border of radicals to change their sizes
  • Hover on the radicals and press BACKSPACE to delete them
  • Press the save button to save your design
