    First Goal

    If we think of strokes as the alphabet of Chinese, what would Chinese look like?

    Our first goal is using strokes to "spell" the radicals, which are part of characters that have their own meanings. Wait a minute, are we talking about root words?

    Instructions

  • In the stroke buttons, look for buttons with the same strokes as the middle of the screen
  • Press stroke buttons to get the strokes
  • Drag strokes to the right place
  • Drag the border of strokes to change their sizes (Hover on strokes and press SHIFT to see the borders)
  • Hover on the strokes and press BACKSPACE to delete strokes
  • Press the hand buttons on the right to get to the next radical
