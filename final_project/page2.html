Second Goal

It's starting to get a little boring. We didn't come here for a Chinese class. What if we replace the strokes with patterns? That sounds fun!

The second goal is using the strokes to build the radicals we just learned. On top of that, replace strokes with patterns to design your own radicals!

Instructions

  • Press stroke buttons to get the strokes
  • Drag strokes to "spell" a radical
  • Double click strokes to replace them with patterns
  • Drag the border of strokes/patterns to change their sizes (Hover on strokes/patterns and press SHIFT to see the borders)
  • Hover on the strokes and press BACKSPACE to delete strokes
  • Press the save button to save your design
